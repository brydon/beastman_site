National COVID-19 Simulations

This page contains forecasting of the COVID-19 pandemic for the whole of Canada. Starred points on the figures represent real data points as reported by the Center for Systems Science and Engineering at Johns Hopkins University. Solid lines represent the projection of the pandemic data as predicted by the mathematical model.

Below, the social distancing strategy can be specified by choosing the proportion of people adhering to social distancing, the time when social distancing began, and the length of time for which social distancing lasts.

Warning: The Data presented below are just simulations with artificially chosen parameters as a proof of concept.
