Research

In this page I discuss my research interests, publications, and presentations.

Research Interests

My research interests are fundamentally linked by my long-time interest in computing and my desire to understand the world mathematically. As a result my research, even the pure mathematics research I have produced, has all been fundamentally linked to the marriage between mathematics and computing. Currently I am interested in how computation and mathematics can be used to understand biological problems, in particular I am interested in using the tools of Machine Learning and Mathematical Models (PDEs, ODEs, etc.) to answer questions of a biological nature.
